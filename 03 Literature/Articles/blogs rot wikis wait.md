# blogs rot. wikis wait.

> [!abstract] Summary
> A poem on the different mindsets when making a wiki vs. a blog.
## Highlights
---
> wiki is my abyss

> **wikis are companions**
> **a ==wiki for you==**-- 
> just you

> **==a blog== is a storefront** 
> pretty products adorned, designed, 
> pristine and tailored, **==for them==**

> blogs rot. 
> wikis wait.
